Tactico

Welcome to Tactico, a new tic tac toe experience!

Players will take turns as attackers and defenders. First, the defender secretly chooses a square to block. Then the attacker attempts to claim an empty square. If the attacker chooses the same square that the defender chose, the attacker has been blocked; no squares are placed and the players switch roles. The first player to place three squares in a line or place five squares total wins! If both players have four squares placed and neither player has three squares in a line, the game is a tie.

Will you go for the obvious move for a quick win or will you try to set up a stronger position for next turn? More importantly, do you know what your opponent will do? Sign in to play now!
